School Church

was estabilished in 1978

The purposes of the Assembly of God Church School are to provide a high standard of education under Christian leadership of The Assembly of God Church for Christian children and children of all communities, recognizing and maintaining that the guiding principle of the governing character and discipline of the School shall be based on Christian teaching. The School shall, at the discretion of the Board of Management, and as it is able, endeavor to make education available to those children whose parents cannot otherwise afford to send them to School. School library

was estabilished in 1980

At One World International School (OWIS), the library stands as a vibrant hub of intellectual discovery. Overflowing with diverse collections spanning genres and languages, it ignites curiosity and cultivates a passion for learning in students of all ages. Adorned with cozy reading nooks and equipped with modern technology, it seamlessly merges tradition with innovation. Knowledgeable librarians curate resources and inspire exploration, fostering a sense of inquiry and critical thinking. Amidst the hustle and bustle of school life, the OWIS library offers a serene retreat, where minds roam freely and imaginations take flight, enriching the educational journey of every student who walks through its doors.

School auditorium

was estabilished in 1982

The OWIS school auditorium stands as a testament to the school's commitment to holistic education and community engagement. With its spacious seating and state-of-the-art acoustics, it serves as a versatile space for a myriad of events, from captivating performances to thought-provoking presentations. Equipped with cutting-edge audiovisual technology, it ensures every word and note resonates with clarity and impact. From student-led dramas and musical recitals to guest lectures and assemblies, the auditorium fosters creativity, confidence, and collaboration among OWIS students. It's not merely a venue but a stage where talents shine, ideas flourish, and memories are made, enriching the vibrant tapestry of the OWIS experience.
